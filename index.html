Japanese Garden

Tranquil Beauty

Kyoto, Japan

A traditional Japanese garden, often found in temples or private residences, designed for contemplation and aesthetic pleasure, featuring elements like ponds, rocks, and carefully pruned trees.

Eiffel Tower

Symbol of Paris

Paris, France

A wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It was constructed from 1887–1889 as the entrance to the 1889 World's Fair.

Statue of Liberty

Icon of Freedom

New York City, USA

A colossal neoclassical sculpture on Liberty Island in New York Harbor, designed by Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi and dedicated on October 28, 1886.

Colosseum

Ancient Roman Amphitheater

Rome, Italy

An oval amphitheatre in the centre of the city of Rome, Italy. Built of travertine limestone, tuff, and brick-faced concrete, it was the largest amphitheatre ever built.

Mount Fuji

Japan's Sacred Peak

Honshu, Japan

An active stratovolcano that is the highest mountain in Japan, standing at 3,776.24 meters (12,389.2 ft). It is a well-known symbol of Japan and is frequently depicted in art and photographs.
